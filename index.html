My career - a synopsis

       2015 - Present

GlamourOz Dolls

I launched the award winning GlamourOZ Dolls Collection.                                                   

         2011

First Solo Exhibition

I had my first solo art exhibition in Sydney, November 2011. Guest speaker at the launch night was writer, artist and cartoonist, Colleen Doran (pictured).

                 2000

Designer, Sydney Olympic Mascots
SOCOG

The 2000 Sydney Mascots, Ollie, Syd and Millie – three native Australian animals that represent earth, air and water – were designed by me and Matt Hatton. I also created the original three-dimensional physical sculptures/maquettes of these mascots.

  1989 - 2001

Senior Character Animator
Disney Animation Studios

At Walt Disney Animation Australia, I was a principal animator on a series of films and TV series. animating characters such as Jasmine and Aladdin, and Ariel and Prince Eric.
